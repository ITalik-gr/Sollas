Frequently asked questions
Is there a possible delay?

We tailor our design delivery to match the scale of your project, with options available within 7 or 14 days. Any potential delays are typically encountered in a single scenario: if you opting to pivot the direction of your product, which would necessitate a complete redesign from the ground up.

Rest assured, as a design agency specializing in 7 or 14-day delivery, we're committed to providing efficient and impactful solutions for your design needs.

How to get started?

Initiating the process is easy. Simply complete the provided form, and our dedicated team will promptly reach out to you. During this interaction, we'll delve into the specifics of your project and outline the subsequent steps to ensure a smooth progression. As a design agency renowned for our 7-day delivery, we're committed to swiftly transforming your ideas into impactful designs.

What if the design doesn't meet my expectations?

Your satisfaction is our priority. Our design team boasts extensive expertise in UI/UX, with over 5 years of experience. We employ a comprehensive approach, gathering abundant information to craft top-tier product designs aligned with your business objectives and user preferences.

However, if by any chance you're not entirely content, we're committed to collaborating closely with you to make necessary adjustments until we achieve a design that not only meets but exceeds your expectations. But to conduct this process we have to resubmit our contract with you

What will be delivered upon project completion?

At the conclusion of your project, you will receive a comprehensive Figma file encompassing all design flows, assets, and a meticulously structured design system. This file will be accompanied by clear instructions to facilitate your seamless integration of the design into your workflow. In addition, we extend complimentary support for a duration of 14 days following the conclusion of the design sprint.

Are there any refunds if I don't like the service?

Once the design has been completed and work has commenced, issuing a refund becomes unfeasible. It's important to note that our track record speaks volumes: every one of our past clients has experienced complete satisfaction with both the communication process and the final results. We're dedicated to ensuring a seamless and productive collaboration, working diligently to address your needs and preferences throughout the design journey.

How I can be involved in design process

Engaging with the design process is a collaborative effort. To keep you involved, we provide access to a Kanban board showcasing the ongoing progress. Additionally, for real-time interaction and enhanced collaboration, you have the option to add our team to your Slack workspace. This not only facilitates swift feedback exchange but also contributes to refining the design process based on your insights, ensuring that the final product aligns seamlessly with your vision and objectives.

Prepare to be amazed by how swiftly your ideas can come to life.
